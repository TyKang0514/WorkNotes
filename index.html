  • Can We Predict the Winner of a Match in World Cup?
    Analysis of World Cup matches throughout history

    Data: World Cup Match Results (1930 - 2022)
    Tools Used: Python, Tableau

    There is always one of our friends saying, 'Let's bet on the match!'

  • Are Coastal Homes Really More Expensive?
    An Application of Inferential Statistics Using T-Tests

    Data: 1990 California Housing Prices
    Tools Used: Python(pandas, scipy, matplotlib)

    If we want to understand something about a group — like how expensive houses are — we usually rely on a sample. That’s where inferential statistics come in, and the t-test is one commonly used method. In this project, t-tests are used to examine housing prices in specific areas, based on the 1990 California housing dataset.
